Service 01

Content Architecture & Strategy

The strategic foundation that every subsequent location inherits. We define the page taxonomy, SEO keyword strategy, brand voice guide, and content differentiation rules for your entire platform. This is the work that makes scaling possible without sacrificing quality.

Most content creation companies skip this step and jump straight to production. That's how you end up with 50 websites that all read the same. The architecture stage ensures every location has a clear, differentiated content mandate before a single word is written.

Scope This Service →

What's included

  • Page taxonomy — every page type each location needs (8–15 pages)
  • SEO keyword strategy per page type with intent mapping
  • Brand voice and editorial style guide
  • Content differentiation rules across locations
  • Anti-pattern library (phrases and structures to avoid)
  • First location fully produced as the quality benchmark

Ideal for

Platforms that have never formalized their content strategy, are migrating to a new CMS, or have existing content that's underperforming in local search. This is the prerequisite for multi-location content creation at scale.

  • Local keyword research — primary, secondary, and long-tail targets
  • Content briefs with location-specific differentiation mandates
  • Full page set (8–15 pages): homepage, about, service pages, provider bios, contact
  • Meta titles and descriptions optimized for local search
  • Schema.org LocalBusiness structured data
  • Cross-site duplicate content QA (<15% similarity threshold)
  • CMS-ready delivery in your preferred format

Service 02

Multi-Location SEO Content Creation

The core production service. Using your content architecture (or one we build), we produce locally differentiated, SEO-optimized website content for 10 to 250+ locations.

Every location gets its own keyword research, its own content brief, and its own unique copy. We don't swap city names into a template — we produce content that reads as if a local copywriter wrote it for each individual practice.

This is what separates our content creation service from agencies that charge $200K and take 3 months, and from content mills that deliver the same page 50 times with different city names.
