The Challenge

Aesthetics content requires a different voice.

MedSpa website content operates at the intersection of medical credibility and luxury positioning. Patients researching Botox in Scottsdale have different expectations than those searching for a family dentist. The content needs to convey clinical expertise while reflecting the aspirational, high-end experience these practices deliver.

Multi-location aesthetics platforms face an additional challenge: treatment menus vary by location depending on equipment, provider specializations, and local demand. A location in Miami may emphasize body contouring and skin tightening. A location in Denver may focus on injectables and facial rejuvenation. The content can't be identical.

Our content creation service for aesthetics practices produces treatment-specific pages that balance medical authority with the sophisticated, conversion-oriented voice that drives consultation bookings.

Typical aesthetics page types

  • Homepage & practice overview
  • Provider bios with credentials & specialties
  • Botox & neurotoxin treatments
  • Dermal fillers (lip, cheek, jawline)
  • Laser skin resurfacing
  • Body contouring (CoolSculpting, Emsculpt)
  • Skin tightening & radiofrequency
  • Chemical peels & facials
  • IV therapy & wellness
  • Financing & membership programs
  • Consultation booking
