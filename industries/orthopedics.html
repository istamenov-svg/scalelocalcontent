The Challenge

Orthopedic content is condition-driven.

Orthopedic patients search differently than dental or primary care patients. They search by condition ("knee pain"), by procedure ("ACL reconstruction"), or by body part ("shoulder specialist"). The content architecture must reflect all three search patterns while accounting for which surgeons at each location specialize in which procedures.

Multi-location orthopedic groups also face a surgeon-branding challenge. Many patients choose their orthopedic surgeon — not their orthopedic practice. Provider pages need to be substantial, credential-rich, and optimized for the surgeon's name plus specialty plus location. These aren't afterthought bios; they're primary landing pages.

Our content creation service for orthopedic platforms builds a content architecture that covers conditions, procedures, body regions, and surgeon profiles — all localized per practice and optimized for how patients in each market actually search.

Typical orthopedic page types

  • Practice homepage & overview
  • Surgeon profiles with fellowship details
  • Joint replacement (knee, hip, shoulder)
  • Sports medicine & arthroscopy
