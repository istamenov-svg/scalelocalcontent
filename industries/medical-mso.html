The Challenge

MSOs have a content complexity problem.

Management services organizations face a content challenge that DSOs and single-specialty platforms don't: variable service mixes across locations. One clinic may offer primary care and behavioral health. Another may be urgent care only. A third might combine internal medicine, pediatrics, and women's health under one roof.

This means the page taxonomy can't be identical across locations. Each site needs a content architecture that reflects what that specific clinic actually offers — and keyword research that targets how patients in that specific market search for those specific services.

Our content creation service for MSOs builds a flexible content architecture that adapts per location while maintaining brand consistency across the platform. Every practice gets the pages it needs, optimized for the services it offers, targeting the keywords patients in its market are searching.

Typical MSO page types

  • Practice homepage & overview
  • Provider directory & individual bios
  • Primary care services
  • Urgent care / walk-in services
  • Specialty service pages (variable per location)
  • Behavioral health / wellness
  • Insurance accepted & billing
  • New patient registration
  • Telehealth services
  • Contact & hours per location
